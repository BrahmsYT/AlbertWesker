About AlbertWesker

What is AlbertWesker?

AlbertWesker is a domain security scanning platform that helps you assess and monitor the security posture of any domain. We provide comprehensive threat analysis, vulnerability detection, and security recommendations.

Features

  • Domain Analysis: Complete security scan of any domain with detailed findings.
  • Risk Assessment: Risk scoring from 0-100 to identify threats quickly.
  • Port Scanning: Detection of open ports and running services.
  • History Tracking: Keep track of all your scans and results.
  • English Only: Clean, focused interface in English.

How It Works

Simply enter a domain name in the scanner, and AlbertWesker will:

  • Perform comprehensive security analysis
  • Check for known vulnerabilities
  • Scan for open ports and services
  • Generate a risk score
  • Provide detailed recommendations

Privacy & Security

Your data is important to us. All scans are performed securely, and we do not store your domain scan details on our servers for longer than necessary. Your scanning history is maintained locally and can be exported anytime.
